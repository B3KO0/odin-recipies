Cabonara

Delicious authentic Italian Cabonara that will leave you wanting more!

Ingridients

  • Spagetti
  • Cabonara sauce
  • Parmegan cheese
  • Bacon

Method

  1. Cook Spagetti in boiling water
  2. Cook bacon in frying pan
  3. Add cooked spagetti to large pot
  4. Add bacon to spagetti
  5. Stir untill well mixed
  6. Serve with parmegan to liking
  7. Enjoy!
