Satay Chicken

Succulent Satay Chicken, that melts in your mouth with every bite!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Satay Sauce
  • Cashew nuts
  • Packet serving of Rice
  • Milk
  • Peas and Corn

Method

  1. Cook chicken in fry pan until white on all sides
  2. Add 1 Bottle of satay sauce
  3. Add milk to taste and consistency
  4. mix well
  5. Add 1Cup Cashew nuts
  6. Add peas and corn
  7. Mix until well coated
  8. Cook rice per package instructions
  9. Serve seperatley and enjoy!
